How It Works

From Brief to Dataset in Days

A streamlined four-step pipeline designed for engineering teams who need reliable results fast.

01

Project Scoping

You describe your dataset requirements — classes, volume, format, edge cases. We scope and confirm within 24 hours.

02

AI Pre-labeling

Our multi-model AI pipeline automatically pre-annotates your images using the best-fit detection model for your scene type — reducing manual effort by up to 80%.

03

Quality Verification

Every annotation passes multi-level QA review. Consistency checks, edge case validation, and format verification before delivery.

04

Delivery & Support

Dataset delivered in your required format. Revision rounds included. Technical support available post-delivery.
