How It Works

From Brief to Dataset in Days

A four-step pipeline designed for engineering teams who need reliable results fast.

Step 01

Project Scoping

You describe your requirements — classes, volume, format, edge cases. We scope and confirm within 24 hours.

›

AI Pre-labeling

Our multi-model AI pipeline pre-annotates images using the best-fit detection model for your scene type — reducing manual effort by up to 80%.
